Summary

My name is Carl Reim, and I am a Transportation Planning Analyst for Hennepin County, Minnesota. I have a Masters in GIS degree along with a certificate in Data Science and Visualization. I enjoy organizing data and producing maps and visualizations that help organizations make informed decisions with spatial insights.

In my current position, I support transportation planning efforts for Hennepin County, Minnesota by helping staff make use of various data sets to make decisions and prioritize projects. I do this by managing roadway and project inventories, developing spatial analyses and producing maps and graphics. Additionally, I’ve been involved in project management and public outreach.

Previously, I’ve worked in a research center focused on providing remote sensing and GIS support to researchers working in the polar regions of Alaska, Greenland and Antarctica. This work included a wide range of user support and field work.

Outside of work, I enjoy working on projects that will expand my skillset, outdoor recreation and roasting coffee.
