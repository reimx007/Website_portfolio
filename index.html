Summary: Latitude vs various weather patterns

The goal of this project was to determine how weather phenomenon changes at different latitudes, and whether there was any relationship between weather patterns and the location on the earthâ€™s surface. To perform this task, we pulled the weather data from the closest cities to over 500 randomly generated X-Y coordinates. The weather data was obtained from the Open Weather Map API, and the latitude of each was plotted against a specific weather phenomenon. We looked specifically at: temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed.

This site summarizes the findings by displaying the graphs that were produced. In addition to each graph produced, the original weather data for each city can be found on the Data page. Enjoy! And maybe you can get some ideas on where to travel for your next vacation!
